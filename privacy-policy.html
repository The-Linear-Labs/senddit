Privacy-First Design

SENDD IT is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information that can identify you, such as your name, email address, phone number, or contact information.

What We Collect

We collect only anonymous usage statistics to improve the app (e.g., which features are used, app crashes). This data cannot be linked back to you personally.

What We Don't Collect

• No pickup lines you generate
• No conversations or messages
• No contacts or personal content
• No identifiable information

Your settings are stored only on your device.

Clipboard Function

Generated messages are copied to your device's clipboard for easy sharing. This happens locally on your device - we don't see or store this content.

Data Retention

Anonymous usage data is kept for up to 6 months to improve the app, then permanently deleted.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, most data protection rights don't apply. However, if you believe we hold any of your personal information, you can contact us at linus_kaiser@outlook.de.
